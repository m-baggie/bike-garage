### US-003: Show estimated cost range per part in pricing column
Status: in_progress
Depends on: US-001

Description:
As a user, I want to see an estimated cost range for each part so I know what to budget even when Performance Bicycle has no results.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] When a part has no Performance Bicycle scraping results, the pricing cell shows the Claude estimated range: 'Est. $15 – $80' in a neutral gray style
- [ ] When scraping results ARE available, show the scraped results first, then below them show 'Claude estimate: $15 – $80' in a smaller muted style
- [ ] If estimated_cost_min and estimated_cost_max are both 0, show 'Cost unknown' instead of 'Est. $0 – $0'
- [ ] Cost range formatted as: 'Est. $[min] – $[max]' with dollar sign and no decimals
- [ ] npm run lint passes
- [ ] Example: Leather Saddle with no scraping results → pricing cell shows 'Est. $15 – $80'
- [ ] Negative case: part with cost_min=0 and cost_max=0 → shows 'Cost unknown', not 'Est. $0 – $0'
